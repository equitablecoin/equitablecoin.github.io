EquitableCoin


For a decentralised, fair and efficient cryptocurrency





Social cryptocurrency!

We believe that miners are important, but users as well. Every time you make a transaction using EquitableCoin, you get a chance to receive 5% of the next block reward.





Fast cryptocurrency!

Blocks can be proceeded up to one per minute. Transactions are confirmed faster and rewards are more frequent for the users and the miners.





Lightweight cryptocurrency!

Based on the Scrypt encryption and on the LTC optimisations, EquitableCoin is lighter than Bitcoin and some of its derivates. EquitableCoin is as safe as Bitcoin.
